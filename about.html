About

Y&K Consulting is a comprehensive consulting company that advises the entire process of drug development from screening to marketing strategies.

With our expertise and experience in non-clinical, clinical, and quality assurance are committed to providing advice on new drug development. We also provide recruiting and training services, patent and legal advice, and investment. We provide consultation and advisory services for all areas of biological products from new drugs to generic and medical devices.

Y&K can help you make your drug development process safe and successful.

  • • The partner with all the resources clients need: Planning, Execution, Staffing, Training, Partnering, IP, Legal and Investment
